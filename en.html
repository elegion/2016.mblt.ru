April 28, Digital October

International Mobile Conference

About

5th International conference is devoted to mobile technologies. In keeping with the best traditions speakers from Silicon Valley, Europe, Japan, and Russia will discuss the trends and innovations in mobile industry. Participants will listen to the success stories, practical cases for businesses, enjoy great networking and much more. Don’t miss a chance to have a great experience! See you at MBLT16!

Conference Report

Photo
Slides

The 5th mobile conference MBLT16 was held on April 28th. There were 666 attendees.

The agenda was divided into two streams. More than 30 speakers took to the stages with presentations and led panel discussions and open interviews called MBLT Talks to share their knowledge and experience. Among them were representatives from such companies as Facebook, Google, Alibaba Mobile Business Group, QuantaCorp, The Economist, Aviasales.

The main topics were global trends, innovations, advertising, analytics, business success stories from international leaders and practical cases for different business spheres. Activities at booths and awesome afterparty from Pure created a great opportunity for networking.

First keynote speaker was Marvin Liao, Partner at 500 Startups. He did presentation "Global investment trends in mobile". Marvin admitted, that the most interesting and perspective IT spheres are VR, IoT, cybersecurity and education. This topic was expanded by Ragnar Sass, co-founder of the biggest European startup accelerator Garage48and also co-founder of CRM Pipedrive:

«Do the project in the sphere that inspires you, after all it will be on your mind 24/7», — said Ragnar.

Engineer and futurist thinker, co-founder of QuantaCorp Nell Watson presented and also showed the example of the role of intelligent machines in our everyday lives. She took to the stage in Lechal shoes, that with the vibrations helps to navigate easily the new city. Shoes are connected with smartphone, on which the actual root is built.

At once three representatives of Facebook presented at MBLT16. Tom Laster, Partner and Ecosystem Development Manager, told the participants about the marketing partnership opportunities with social network. Anna-Maria Treneva spoke about launching of the new flexible targeting platform and gave the tips for smarter ads settings on Facebook. Clive Rian, Regional Director of Northern and Central Europe Sales and Client Management, mentioned in his speech that...

... 8 billions videos are being posted on Facebook every day.

Tigran Khudaverdyan from Yandex.taxi announced Telegram bot launching. Managing Partner at Rambler&Co Andrew Ilichev told the listeners about the plans of implementing the new function like Shazam to Rambler Kassa app. It would detect movie trailers and add them to the wish list, then it would send notifications about the dates of releases.

Andrew Bakalenko, Head of Mobile Products at Sberbank, shared the success story of Sberbank Online mobile app, gave the details on the process, instruments and approaches they'd been using during 4 years.

The result was 13,2 millions of users and the "Best app 2015" reward in App Store and Google Play.

Also representatives of app stores participated at MBLT16. Alexey Kokin, Partner Development Manager at Google Play, gave the tips how to make the app of high quality, shared how Google Play featured apps and put them in the TOP and also answered loads of questions about user acquisition.

Jamie Credland, Global Head of Client Marketing & Research at British newspaper the Economist, shared how they launched a new mobile app for morning news Espresso.

According to their researches, 17% of people starts the morning with coffee and just 10% say "good morning" at first. However 35% of people take their phones in hands at the first minute of awakening.

Gathering such user data helps the Economist to update the content in their app and thus make it irreplaceable for users. The last talk was really frankly presentation from Elena Rydkina — sex evangelist in Pure, fast dating app. Elena told about trends and innovations in SexTech.

"We organize this conference for the 5th time. Each time the content is the top priority task for us. And this year we managed to gather representatives from Facebook, Google, 500Startups, Alibaba, QuantaCorp and many other keynote speakers at one place. Exactly such high level of conference's agenda makes MBLT unique. It's pleasure to see that every year MBLT hosts more and more loyal attendees. MBLT16 became the biggest one among our conferences ever", — commented Roman Beloded, CEO at e-Legion.
